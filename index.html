Sight++ APP is a Voice Guidence designed for visually-impaired people


  • Easy operation for visually-impaired people

  • Provide a portable Intel sense camera as the detection tool for voice guidence (also a NUC computer for helping the operation of the camera will be provided)

  • Use more advanced CV technology to better recognise the environment for visually-impaired people

  • Securely and automatically connect to the suitable Intel cameras for detection

  • The system support the multiple cameras to work together







Simple UI For Users

Only one buttons on the UI including the functions of Setting and Voice Obstacle Avoidence - easy for visually-impaired people to operate.



Intelligent Connection

APP will connect to the wearable NUC computer and intel sense cameras for detecting the obtacles and objects to do the voice guidence for visually-impaired people, and more importantly the system support the multiple cameras to work together.

Moreover the APP can also connect with some static location Intel cameras in different places.
